Sweet Potato Casserole
Home
Ingredients
  • (1) Egg
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • (2) 48oz cans of Bruce's canned yams (Or 5-6 cooked sweet potatoes)
  • (1) tsp. vanilla
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 stick margarine

Topping

  • 1/2 cup light brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup chopped pecans
  • (1) stick margarine
Directions

Casserole

  • Mix all ingredients together and pour into casserole dish.

Topping

  • Mix all together over low heat, cook until mixed.
  • Put on top of casserole
  • Bake at 300Â° for 30 minutes or until top is brown.

Note: I Usually double topping recipe
