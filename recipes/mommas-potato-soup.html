Momma's Potato Soup
Home
Ingredients
  • (1)lb Bacon
  • (2) 32oz pkg frozen hashbrowns (shredded or diced)
  • (3) 14oz cans chicken broth
  • (3) regular cans of cream of chicken Soup
  • (2) 8oz pkg cream cheese (softened and cut up)
  • (1) tbs parsley flakes
  • (1) tbs onion powder
Directions
  • Cook bacon, drain and crumble. Set aside
  • In at least a 5qt Crockpot combine all other ingredients
  • Stir in bacon and cook on high for 4-5 hours or on low for 8-10 hours
