Collard Greens
Home
Ingredients
  • (1/2) pound smoked meat (ham hocks, turkey wings, or neck bones)
  • (1) cup salt
  • (1/4) cup black pepper
  • (1/4) cup garlic powder
  • (1) tbs seasoned salt (Lawry's)
  • (1) tbs Texas Pete hot sauce
  • (1) large bunch of collards
  • (8) tbs (1 stick) of butter
Directions
  • In a large pot, bring (3) quarts of water to a boil
  • Add smoked meat, seasonings, and hot sauce
  • Reduce heat to medium and cook for (1) hour
  • In the meantime, wash collard green and remove the center stem
  • Stack 6-8 stripped leaves on top of eachother and roll them up
  • Slice into 1/2 to 1 inch thick slices
  • Place greens in pot, add butter after greens
  • Cook for (45)-(60) minutes stirring occasionally
