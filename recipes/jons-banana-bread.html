Jon's Banana Bread
Home
Ingredients
  • (3) ripe bananas
  • (1/2 cup) melted butter
  • (1/2 cup) white sugar
  • (1/2 cup) brown sugar
  • (2) eggs
  • (1/2 cup) milk
  • (1 tsp) vanilla
  • (1 1/2 cup) flour
  • (1 tsp) baking powder
  • (1 tsp) baking soda
  • (1/4 tsp) salt
  • (1/4 tsp) cinnamon
  • (1/2 cup) walnuts
  • (2 squares) 85% dark chocolate
Directions
  • Mash bananas into bowl
  • Add ingredients in order until flour, into a bowl, then mix together
  • Add (1 1/2 cup) flour
  • Add (1 tsp) baking powder
  • Add (1 tsp) baking soda
  • Add (1/4 tsp) salt
  • Add (1/4 tsp) cinnamon
  • Add (1/2 cup) walnuts
  • Add (2 squares) dark chocolate
  • Pour into bread pan
  • Bake (355°F) for (45 minutes)
  • Move to bottom rack at (345°F) for (10–20 minutes)
