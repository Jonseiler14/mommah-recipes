Mamaw Short's Chocolate Dumplings
Home
Ingredients
  • (1) lb biscuit dough
  • (2) cups sugar
  • (5) tbs cocoa
  • (2) cups water
  • (1) stick margarine
  • (1) small can of evaporated milk
Directions
  • Mix all ingredients except dough in a large sauce pan
  • Stir well and bring to boil
  • Roll biscuit dough out until flat
  • Tear off pieces and drop into boiling chocolate mixture
  • Cook until dumplings are firm
