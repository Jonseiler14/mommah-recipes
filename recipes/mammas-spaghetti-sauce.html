Mamma's Spaghetti Sauce
Home
Ingredients
  • (5) lbs ground beef
  • (2) tbsp minced garlic
  • (1) cup low sodium beef broth
  • (2) 28oz cans crushed tomatoes
  • (1) 4oz can tomato paste
  • (1) jar marinara sauce
  • (1) tsp oregano
  • (1) tsp ground thyme
  • (4) whole bay leaves
  • (2) tbsp sugar
  • (2) tsp salt
  • Pinch of crushed red pepper to your liking
  • (3) tbsp parsley flakes
  • (1/2) cup grated parmesan cheese (optional)
Directions
  • Brown meat, then drain
  • Add all other ingredients and beat in large pot, mix and simmer at least (1) hour

Notes:

  • Dont eat the bay leaves!
