Mississippi Pot Roast
Home
Ingredients
  • (1) chuck roast (approx. 3–6 lbs)
  • (1) ranch packet or (3) tbsp dry ranch seasoning
  • (1) brown gravy packet
  • (1/2) cup butter (1 stick)
  • (10–12) pepperoncini peppers
  • (1/2) cup pepperoncini juice from jar
Directions
  • Place roast in Crockpot
  • Sprinkle ranch and brown gravy seasoning on top
  • Add pepperoncini peppers and juice, then place stick of butter on top of roast
  • Cook on low (8–10) hours or high (6–7) hours
