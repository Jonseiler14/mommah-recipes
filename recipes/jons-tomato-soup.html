Jon's Tomato Soup
Home
Ingredients
  • (8) Roma tomatoes
  • (1 large) red onion
  • (1–2 heads) garlic
  • Olive oil to coat
  • Salt and pepper
  • (3 tsp) sugar
  • (1 tbsp) cayenne pepper
  • (1 tsp) nutmeg
  • (1 tbsp) balsamic vinegar
  • (1 cup) chicken stock
  • (1/2 cup) heavy cream
  • Basil leaves
Directions
  • Cut tomatoes in half
  • Cut onion into fourths
  • Cut nubs off garlic
  • Place on baking sheet
  • Drizzle with olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Place in (350°F) oven for (20–25 minutes)
  • Place roasted veggies in a pot or blender
  • Add (1 cup) chicken stock
  • Add a few leaves of basil
  • Add (1/2 tbsp) cayenne pepper
  • Add (1 tsp) nutmeg
  • Add (1 tbsp) balsamic vinegar
  • Blend while adding (1/2 cup) heavy cream
  • Add heavy cream and basil to garnish
