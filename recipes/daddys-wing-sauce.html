Daddy's Wing Sauce
Home
Ingredients
  • (1) cup Texas Pete
  • (1) scorpion pepper (stem removed)
  • (2)tbs honey
  • (1)tbs ground mustard
  • (1)tbs butter
  • (1)tsp cumin
  • (1)tbs minced garlic
  • (1)tsp paprika
  • (1)tbs chili powder
Directions
  • Mix all ingredients into pot
  • Bring to simmer
